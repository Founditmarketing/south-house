Nobody wants an unexpected bill. Two straightforward billing approaches, estimated before we begin.

Monthly Retainer

Ideal for ongoing engagements. After an initial interview, we estimate the scope and propose a monthly amount. If the scale changes, we renegotiate — always fair, always transparent.

Per-Engagement

For most individual clients. We review prior returns, conduct a brief interview, and provide an estimate during or shortly after the initial consultation. One invoice upon completion.

Common Questions

Straight answers

I don't live near Knoxville. Can you still help me?

+
Absolutely. SouthHouse CPA has been fully remote since 2017 and currently serves clients across the U.S. and internationally — including Spain, Switzerland, Hong Kong, and St. Kitts. All documents are exchanged through a secure portal, and meetings happen virtually.

Will anyone else work on my account?

+
No. Every piece of work on your account is completed personally by Steven Sorhus, CPA. There is no back office, no associates, and no hand-offs. The person you speak with is the person who prepares your returns.

How quickly will you respond to my calls or emails?

+
SouthHouse CPA does not use a secretary or answering service. Your calls and emails go directly to Steven's cell phone and are answered as soon as practical — typically the same day.

How do I know what I'll pay before we start?

+
After an initial interview and review of your situation, you'll receive a clear estimate. No work begins until both parties agree on scope and fee. If circumstances change mid-engagement, we renegotiate openly.

I received a suspicious letter from the IRS. Can you help?

+
Yes. Business owners are frequently targeted by phishing attacks and compliance scams disguised as legitimate IRS correspondence. We help you identify what's real and what isn't, and handle legitimate IRS notices directly on your behalf.
